ABOUT US

Florex Sales is a floral industry marketplace that was created for wholesalers to connect their clients with growers from around the world, allowing easy access to one of the largest flower availabilities in North America.

We offer the most innovative piece of software the flower industry has had to date.

For this project, we got together with growers, wholesalers, event planners and retail florists, and we created a tool that allows you and your clients to order exactly what’s needed, when it’s needed. You and your clients can connect with over 300 growers from over 10 countries from around the world. Our tool allows you and your clients to order by variety, color, and even grower. Assortments of varieties can be combined in the same box, simplifying the procurement process and solving the problem of over ordering and over stocking, no need to over order, you get exactly what you want, when you want it, as simple as that. No order is too small, no order is too big!
