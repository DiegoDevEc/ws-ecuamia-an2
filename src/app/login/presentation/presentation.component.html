ABOUT US




Florex Market Place is obsessed with providing an outstanding user

experience by simplifying the buying and selling process between

flower producers, importers, distributors, and floral decorators.

With over 5000 varieties of flowers to choose from, our platform

grants users the ability to order exactly what they want, when they

want it. Users are able to order solid or custom mix boxes from

origin saving time and the hassle of overstocking.



Our mission is to help maximize sales exponentially for our users

while saving time and having a worry free ordering experience.
